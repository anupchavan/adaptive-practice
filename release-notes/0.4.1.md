## Adaptive Practice 0.4.1

Fixes from live 0.4.0 use, focused on large sessions and daily coverage.

### Large sessions no longer hit output token limits

- A 20-question request could exceed the output ceiling shared by every provider (~8K tokens), truncating mid-JSON — reason-rich options simply don't fit in one response. Sessions above 8 questions now generate in **balanced chunks** (e.g. 7 + 7 + 6) behind the scenes and merge, so no single request can truncate, on any provider. Per-question output budget also increased to match the richer option format.

### Daily practice always blends old and new

- Previously, a review backlog could fill the entire daily topic limit and shut new notes out completely. Daily sessions now **reserve up to two slots for never-practiced notes** whenever they exist — you stay in touch with new material every day, and the displaced reviews remain due and come back tomorrow. The existing cap (at most three new notes once reviews exist) still protects review debt from novelty.
- The **daily topic limit now goes up to 30** (was 12). Note that every selected note's content is sent to the model, so higher limits increase per-session token use.

### Under the hood

- Chunked generation reuses the flow engine's batch machinery, so every chunk still passes the full calibration, difficulty-verification, and repair pipeline, and duplicate stems across chunks are filtered.
